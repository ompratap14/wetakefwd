FAQ

Common Questions

What industries do you work with? +
We work across all major industries including e-commerce, healthcare, edtech, fintech, legal, and SaaS. Our AI solutions are industry-agnostic — if you have data and a process, we can automate or augment it with AI.
How long does a typical project take? +
Simple chatbots and automation flows can be delivered in 1–2 weeks. Complex AI agents or full-stack applications typically take 4–8 weeks. We always start with a scoping session to give you a precise timeline before committing.
Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance? +
Yes — all our projects include a 30-day post-launch support window at no extra cost. After that, we offer flexible monthly retainers for ongoing monitoring, model fine-tuning, and feature additions.
Is my business data secure? +
Absolutely. We follow strict data handling protocols — all client data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We sign NDAs before any work begins, and we never use your data to train models without explicit written consent.
Can you integrate AI into my existing software? +
Yes, integration is often where we do our best work. Whether it's adding an AI layer to an existing CRM, ERP, or custom web app — we build APIs and connectors that slot cleanly into your current tech stack.
What is your pricing model? +
We offer project-based pricing for defined scope deliverables and monthly retainers for ongoing partnerships. We're happy to discuss flexible arrangements including milestone-based billing for larger engagements. Book a consultation to get a tailored quote.
